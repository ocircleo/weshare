We share
v 1.0.1

WeShare is an app for sharing all sorts of files between any devices in the same wifi network. It is not bound by any specific platform. The app may not work on WPS or guest networks. At least one laptop or Mac-Book needs to have the app installed in order to make a network connection. The app uses HTTP protocol.
It is 100% safe, no user data is collected. Be cautious of using in public network.
